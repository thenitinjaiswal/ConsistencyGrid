## ğŸ¯ Best Practices for Life Milestones

### 1. **Make Them Significant**
- Not everyday tasks, but major life events
- Events that mark chapters in your life
- Achievements you'll remember in 20 years

### 2. **Set Clear Deadlines**
- Life milestones need timeframes
- Use specific dates, not vague "eventually"
- Plan backwards from deadline

### 3. **Break Into Sub-Goals**
- Create 4-8 meaningful sub-goals
- Each should be a measurable step
- Sub-goals lead to milestone completion

### 4. **Update Regularly**
- Check off sub-goals as completed
- Update progress frequently
- Stay motivated by seeing progress

### 5. **Balance Your Life**
- Mix categories for holistic growth
- Include milestones across all life areas
- Don't just focus on work or wealth
